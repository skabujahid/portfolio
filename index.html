SK. Abu Jahid

I am an Environmental Scientist who combines eco-hydrology, eco-socialism, disaster vulnerability and climate change to investigate the conditions, changes, and processes. Drawing on political ecology and sustainability, I make use of both quantitative and qualitative analysis to investigate environment governance issues around the globe.
My interests center around:
  • how the environmental flow of rivers changes over time due to anthropogenic interventions.
  • how the eco-socialism evolves for climate change politics and
  • how the vulnerability can be assesed to investigate the risk of the people.
